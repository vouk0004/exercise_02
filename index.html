Hey, remember this stuff from when we were kids?

My favorite flash game that I would play in the computer lab

Ok so there are a lot of different games that I could list here.
Run, Spank the Monkey, Bowman, Bloons Tower Defense 4 (and then BTD5 once we were in middle school)--I could go on for hours. But the classic game that I keep going back to is Fancy Pants Man, specifically Fancy Pants Adventure 2. A good balance of simple, yet creative mechanics in a really fun-looking world. If you don't remember Fancy Pants Man, you should play it...like right now (obviously on Addicting Games).

My favorite Disney Channel original movie

Again, there are a lot I could list here. The Cheetah Girls, The Thirteenth Year, Smart House, High School Musical (overrated, but still good), and Wendy Wu: Homecoming Warrior are just a few memorable ones (though Smart House was memorable for the wrong reasons). But the Disney Channel Origanal Movie that stands out to me the most is Halloweentown (1-3). These movies are admittedly pretty strange, but as a kid they got me f*cking PUMPED for Halloween. Like, if I didn't watch at least one Halloweentown movie at some point in October, then I'd probably refuse to go Trick-or-Treating. In Return to Halloweentown, they recast Marnie for some reason and honestly, the new actress just didn't have the same charm as the original.
